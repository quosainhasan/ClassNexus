About ClassNexus

ClassNexus is a platform designed to bring students and teachers together. Here, you can access your class timetable, syllabus, class notes, view your attendance record, and much more. Join us today and take control of your learning experience!

Features

  • Timetable - View your weekly timetable
  • Syllabus - Access your course syllabus
  • Class Notes - View and download class notes uploaded by your teacher
  • Attendance - Keep track of your attendance record for each class
  • Profile - View your personal information and QR code
